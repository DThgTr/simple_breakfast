Eggcelent Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small slice of butter (just about any brand is fine)
  • 1 coffee spoons of salt
  • 2 coffee spoons of sugar
  • 2 table spoon of milk
  • 2 slices of SPAM
  • Enough cheese to cover the SPAM slices
  • 2 slices of bread (or 20 of them if you want to build a sandwich tower, but we are gonna need to match the other ingredients in quantity)

Recipe

  1. Put on the pan and stove and remember to use a pan big enough for 2 slices of bread.
    Keep the heat at medium and let the butter slice melt in the pan. Remember to move it around to spread it evenly.

  2. Put the slices of bread on the pan after the butter has melt completely.
    Keep the heat at low or we are gonna eat charcoal for breakfast!

  3. Put the eggs in a bow together with spoons of sugars, salt and milk, then give it a good twirl with a mixing tool of your choice.
    Don't stop till everything is blended together all nice and creamy!

  4. Wait until the bread is somewhat crunchy (it should be after you finish for egg mixture), then put them aside.
    Pour the mixture in and remember to put the uncooked side of the bread face down into the mixture.

  5. Turn the heat up to medium and wait for the eggs mixture to cook.
    And yes! Charcoal warning - keep your eyes on them.

  6. Flip the egg and break pancake around on a disk and fold the excessive size into the breads. Put the cheese and SPAM slices on the bread and fold it.

  7. On the stove once more! Leave the heat at low until the bread slice turn a gold and brownish color.
    Flip the do the same for the other side.

  8. Served hot, best with a nice steamy cup of coffee!
